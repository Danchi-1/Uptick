About Danc Hotels

Nestled in the heart of Abeokuta, Danc Hotels is more than just a place to stay - it's your home away from home. Since our founding in 2015, we have been dedicated to providing comfort, class, and top-tier hospitality to travelers, tourists, and professionals from across Nigeria and beyond.

With elegantly designed rooms, world-class facilities, and a commitment to exceptional service, we aim to make every stay memorable. Whether you're in town for business, leisure, or a weekend getaway, Danc Hotels offers the perfect blend of relaxation and functionality.

Our team is made up of passionate hospitality professionals who understand the importance of warmth and attention to detail. At Danc Hotels, every guest is treated like family.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Do you offer airport pickup and drop-off?
A: Yes, we provide 24/7 airport shuttle services. You can book in advance or at the front desk.

Q: Is the hotel pet-friendly?
A: Unfortunately, we do not accommodate pets at this time.

Q: What time is check-in and check-out?
A: Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability.

Q: Is breakfast included in the room rate?
A: Yes, complimentary breakfast is included for all room bookings.

Q: Do you have a fitness center or gym?
A: Yes, we have a well-equipped fitness center open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.
